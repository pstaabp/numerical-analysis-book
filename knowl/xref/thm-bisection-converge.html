Theorem 3.2.8.

Let \(f\) be a continuous function on the closed interval \([a,b]\) with \(f(a)f(b) \lt 0\text{.}\) The bisection method generates a sequence \(\{p_{n}\}\) which converges to \(p\text{,}\) a root (where \(f(p)=0\) ) within \([a,b]\text{,}\) with the property that
\begin{equation} |p-p_{n}| \leq \frac{b-a}{2^{n}}\tag{3.2.1} \end{equation}
