Theorem 2.3.4.

Let \((p_{n})\) be a sequence that converges to \(p\) and \(\{\beta_{n}\}\) be a sequence that converges to 0. If
\begin{equation*} \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\frac{|p_{n}-p|}{\beta_{n}}= L \end{equation*}
for \(0 \lt L \lt \infty\text{,}\) then \((p_{n})\) converges with \(O(\beta_{n})\text{.}\)
